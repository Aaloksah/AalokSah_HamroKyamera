HOW TECHNOLOGY AEFFECTS DAILY LIFE AND BUSINESS.

Posted by Aalok | May 17, 2023

Introduction:

Technology,in simple words is the use of scientific inventions,ideas and knowledge into the practical life for the purpose of making human life more easier. It has drastically changed today’s world by bringing in new ideas, tools, etc. From the earliest inventions like the wheel and the printing press to the complex digital systems and artificial intelligence of today, technology has played a crucial role in shaping the world we live in. It has revolutionized communication, transportation, healthcare, education, and countless other areas, making our lives more interconnected, convenient, and productive. Technology continues to evolve rapidly, with innovations constantly emerging, pushing the boundaries of what is possible and opening up new avenues for exploration and development.

Positive Impact of Technology on Life & Business:

In our daily life, it has helped us by improving our communication. With the help of technology, we have access to news, information,messages from users from all over the world. The access to information has become very easy and vast. Due to help of technology, we also have a very advanced lifestyle. If we compare our lifestyle to how life was for the people in the past who were living without technology, we can see that we definitely have it easy in a lot of different sectors. Likewise, in the field of business as well, technology plays a very vital role. Businesses all over the world can grow better with the help of technology. Technology gives access to consumers all over the world to have a look at businesses all over the world. To enhance a business, technology is the main factor assisting it. There are a lot of businesses all over the world. If we take an example of a single sector of business, like of food, there are a lot of companies. The competition in that field is pretty tough. Not every business in that sector can be successful. But with the help of technology, a business can definitely be more efficient. Lets take an example: There are a lot of companies all over the world that produce sodas/soft drinks. Among them, CocaCola is one of the most renowned companies in this sector. One of the reasons that the Coca-Cola company is so successful is because of the right use of technology in order to globalize its brand. Globalization is in fact another facility of technology that helps the business to attain a greater reach. Through proper research with the help of technology, the company was able to create skillful advertisements and create proper partnerships with bottling companies and distributors all over the world that helped it to be known so much.

Negative Impact of Technology on Life & Business:

Now, if we talk just about the positive sides of technology, it is like taking a look at one side of a coin. Technology in fact has negative impacts and not just one, multiple of them. Privacy is one of the things that the advancement in technology has degraded. People have less privacy because of having access to the advanced technologies online and offline. People who are addicted to the use of electronic devices have to suffer various physical and mental issues in the long run. Because of the extreme increase in the use of mobile phones, internet among teenagers it has affected their social skills issues as well and reduced creativity too. One of the creations of technology, social media, is something that has become an integral part in most people’s lives. The use of social media among people has increased alot. It has also increased a lot of crimes happening online. Social media is very addictive. It is also very dangerous as some people using it have a lot of negative intentions. Cyberbullying is one of the things that social media is responsible for.

Impact of Technology on selling of Camera equipment:

Selling camera equipment, as the name suggests is a type of business involved in the merchandising of camera equipments. Before the invention of technology, stores that involved in this business had only one source of sales that was physical. Physical sales refers to the purchasing of goods by the customers by physically visiting the stores. But, after the introduction to technology, there was also the introduction of e-commerce.

E-commerce refers to the buying as well as selling of goods and services online through several mediums like websites, apps etc. Ecommerce helps businesses by allowing them a new source of sales, that is online sales. It also allows the business to advertise online about its products and services. In this era of technology, ecommerce is the best method to drive in sales as people find it very convenient to shop online and have the products delivered to their doorstep rather than going out to purchase it physically.

Technology has had a huge impact while selling camera equipment. It has provided a new platform to showcase the products along with their details. With the help of technology, products can also be advertised with the help of several other apps, websites etc which is also the invention of technology. There is also a source through which people can provide reviews and suggest improvements with the help of which the business can take into consideration what to improve next. This way enhancement of the business can take place in a very convenient way. New technology has made it easier for salespeople to capture their target audience. There's a range of techniques, tools and methods that salespeople can use to convert and secure more clients.

Final conclusion:

Hence, technology as a coin, has both bad sides and good sides. How we use it defines whether the good side can overcome the bad side or the bad side will overcome the good side. The advancement in technology is also increasing day by day. New technologies will overcoming the present ones in the future. Technology has indeed enhanced our lives for the better. But it will turn out to be really bad if it is misused.
