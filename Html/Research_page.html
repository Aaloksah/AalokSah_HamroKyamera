Design Inspiration's!

This page highlights our website's design inspiration, which is derived from various original website designs with slight modifications. We give full credit to the respective owners for their exceptional creativity and originality. Our goal is to showcase the diverse range of innovative web designs while acknowledging the remarkable work of the individuals behind the original designs. We have incorporated elements from these inspirations, but the credit for the original designs belongs solely to their respective owners. We invite you to explore our website and appreciate the visual appeal that stems from these talented designers' contributions.



#1


Inspired Design

Our Design

Details: This layout of Product Box has been stimulated from the Apple's official website. The round corner of the box with slight black shadow was so minimal and modern looking that we couldn't resist us from making the same design. This made us to implement this design in our website's Product Page with little bit of changes. At the end the final outcome was really satisfying and i can say that this was the best design out of all other inspired designs.
